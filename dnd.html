Unnamed adventure:

The ideas train

I'm going to use lots of things out of Sandy Petersen's Cthulhu Mythos as I am passionate about Lovecraftian horror and have bought and paid for a hardcopy of this book. Most of the images will be generated by AI, but almost all of the maps will be from Reddit or Pinterest, and I will link to all sources I used. 

Lets begin with the map:

This map was found on this reddit post. I really love that it has many different locations that can influence the story. Also using this website the characters can always have access to it on their phone. 

I think I want this module to be a plug and play, lasting approximately 5 sessions. 
I will add lots of bonus material if I have time and don't get sick of the project. 

I want the story to have a pirate king and a pope, both respectively very powerful politically. And various very different locations. 

This is the main premise I want to play with
The church is hiding a massive Eldridge being underground, which gives them some sinister advantage. It gives them power, but is causing a curse/plague tbd. Maybe people are being driven mad and going missing (called psych, short for phycho) and people who start to physically change, turning into something (called tbd). 
The party must discover the secret, find the vault, and realise that there are 5 or 3 objects that need to be recovered. Hidden around the city by please who are either being payed, extorted or are just friends of the church. The party needs these 3 items to open the vault and defeat (or not) the dragon. 

One is kept in the in the cathedral
One was given to the farmers, but was stolen and lost into the slums 
One was traded by the pirate lord, to the powerful family
One is said to be displayed at the Palace (but its a fake)  

The first thing the party is greeted with is a sign saying Larandel and then has 2 arrows. Upper city and lower city. Lower leads to slums, upper leads to the main city. The Lower city sign is noticeably more tattered and worn than the other. The party can return to this decision at any time. 

But the general beats will be:
Explore - talk to people, find out some people are going mad.
Investigate - find mad people, try and dig a bit deeper, end up in the mines or docks.
Uncover - uncover either the fact that there are certain objects that need to be collected, or that there is something being kept underground.
Collect - collect the items
Fight the beast
