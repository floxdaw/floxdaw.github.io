The Slums

A relatively lawless place, the slums are run by a primarily by the pirate kind and the Powerful Dwarven mining collective. As long as the mines keep producing, the dwarves profit and proclaim to "prop up the economy". And they will do whatever it takes to make sure they keep producing. The pirate kind does use the slums for organised crime profits, but generally has more care and connection to the people of the slums, also providing aid, and protection. The upper city do not consider the slums as part of the city, and hence ignore them as much as possible. The gates to go from the Slums to the upper city are permanently closed. The only other obvious way to get to the upper, is by exiting the city. THe party will learn that there are hidden passages from the mines to the upper as well.  

Many Slum dwellers refer to the slums as the "LC" (sort for lower city). 

The primary races are: Halflings, half orks, humans, dwarves but poor.
Many workers, dwarves particularly, are lost in the mines. The slums are where people are really starting to go mad. 

The main city is open ended. Busy, over crowded, full of good and bad people. Shops, crime, places to be entertained.

The tavern is the main building in the main city.

The mines are the entrance to the underground and are always staffed by the dwarf mining collective employees. 

The hospital is very derelict and becoming more full of people with the Creeping Madness. 

The Tavern

I got this map from Darkest Maps

The tavern is owned and run by Juniper, a human gypsy looking woman. The tavern is more of a square, propped up on stilts and features the main building and a large outdoor patio.  This is where all members of the slums come to unwind, do business - all sorts. Simply speaking to people may lead to quests. 

The party can stay here if they talk to Juniper and she approves and you can agree on a price. She offers you to stay for free, if you complete the adventure hook quest.
