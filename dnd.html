Unnamed adventure:

The ideas train

Lets begin with the map:

This map was found on this reddit post. I really love that it has many different locations that can influence the story. Also using this website the characters can always have access to it on their phone. 

I think I want this module to be a plug and play, lasting approximately 5 sessions. 
I will add lots of bonus material if I have time and don't get sick of the project. 

I want the story to have a pirate king and a pope, both respectively very powerful politically. And various very different locations. 

These will be some of the locations.
  1. Slums. Halflings, half orks, humans
  2. Upper city + cathedral. dragonfolk, elf, human, dwarf, tiefling. Maybe the cathedral is keeping something captive that is starting to somehow effect the city in a bad way.
  3. under city/mines. goblins, humans, orks,  dwarf but poor, deep gnome, dugar
  4. East/west outskirts. famers, halflings, gnomes
  5. Docks. Any
  6. Palace - TBC
These will be some of the characters
  1. Harengon the Wizard. 
  2. Miner
  3. Gypsy/ store owner / guide
  4. Pope (will use another name) 
  5. Master of the docks (pirate) .
