About Me

This is my personal website. I use it for a journal of my projects primarily, but nothing here is private. Feel free to explore and see what I've been up to. 

I have a goal here to make all of my tech projects out of second hand parts and open source software where possible. This often adds a fun extra challenge, and an added element of uncontrollability to the projects. 

I do this to show that cool tech projects don't need a huge budget, and that you can learn many things by working to solve problems under these limitations. I understand that this approach is not always feasible, especially to the time poor, but I have had loads of fun doing these often janky, MacGyvered projects. 

The only downfall of this approach is that there's only so much something can be jury-rigged until it just doesn't work, so sometimes a bit of money has to be spent to make sure things actually work, but with enough prior research, this problem can be minimized.
