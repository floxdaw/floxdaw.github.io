The City

The main city, full of merchants and nobles. 

The main city is open ended. Busy, over crowded, full of good and bad people. Shops, crime, places to be entertained.

The tavern is the main building in the main city.

The mines are the entrance to the underground and are always staffed by the dwarf mining collective employees. 

The hospital is very derelict and becoming more full of people with the Creeping Madness. 

The Tavern

I got this map from Darkest Maps

The tavern is owned and run by Juniper, a human gypsy looking woman. The tavern is more of a square, propped up on stilts and features the main building and a large outdoor patio.  This is where all members of the slums come to unwind, do business - all sorts. Simply speaking to people may lead to quests. 

The party can stay here if they talk to Juniper and she approves and you can agree on a price. She offers you to stay for free, if you complete the adventure hook quest.
